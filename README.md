## [Scuba Warrior](https://phantom-xyz.itch.io/scuba-warrior)

Somewhere at the bottom of the sea, a warrior rises. Fight in the Underwater Colosseum and defeat the champion to become the new champion of the depths. Survive through waves and enemies and challenge all the bosses to make your way as the crowned number one!

## Overview

A wave defense-styled game. Similar combat to Terraria. Waves get progressively harder and the highest wave reached for each difficulty is saved upon death and exiting to the main menu.

Survivalist difficulty = hard difficulty but with no heals.

## Controls

- Left click to fire
- Q to dash
- A/D to move left/right
- S to go under platform
- Space to jump/double jump
